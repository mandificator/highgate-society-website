1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly, remember your preferences, and understand how you use the site.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Fonts, which may set cookies or make requests to Google's servers. These are used solely for loading typefaces and do not track you for advertising purposes.

6. Managing Cookies

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies — all categories are enabled
  • Reject non-essential cookies — only strictly necessary cookies are used
  • Change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.
